Guided, longer-form online study for intellectual and personal growth.

Seminar Ready

A 4-week bridging course for the transition to university-level Literature or Humanities study.

What's Included

  • Four live, small-group online seminars with Dr Lauren Cooper
  • All critical articles and secondary readings provided
  • A course workbook to guide your learning across all four weeks
  • A warm, intimate group capped at 12 students

What You'll Explore

  • Building critical thinking skills and your own analytical voice
  • How to approach a university reading load without overwhelm
  • Contributing confidently in seminar-style discussions
  • Critical thinking and building your own academic voice

Who It's For

  • Students entering their first year of university this September
  • Literature, History, Philosophy, Sociology, or any Humanities student
  • Introverted, sensitive, or neurodivergent learners
  • First-generation, international, or mature students
