my story

why this work matters (for both of us)

My soul's purpose is to make space for sensitive souls like you and I, because our empathy, insights and creativity are important, personally and politically. We can only soften, heal and change this world if we have space to (gently) thrive.

I am honoured to be able to do this work through independent tutoring and my holistic methodology for literary study and self-reflection. I can't wait to connect with you, fellow sensitive soul, and create a sacred space for you, your books and your growth.

Throughout my own struggles, literature has been my constant, getting me through the darkest experiences. As I've spent decades trying to understand and heal myself, journaling and self-care have become just as integral to my existence, and I deeply believe that I am on this planet to share these tools.

I have first-hand experience of navigating the world (and academia) as a highly sensitive and neurodivergent person. The world hasn't been built for us and just existing in it can be draining.
